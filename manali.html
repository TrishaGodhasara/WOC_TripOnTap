Manali & Kasol

Mountains • Snow • Adventure • Cafés

Overview

Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for snowy mountains, adventure sports, scenic valleys and peaceful cafés.

Best Time to Visit

October to March for snow, April to June for pleasant weather.

Top Places to Visit

  • Solang Valley
  • Rohtang Pass
  • Old Manali
  • Hadimba Temple
  • Kasol & Parvati Valley

Adventure Activities

  • Skiing
  • Paragliding
  • River Rafting
  • Trekking

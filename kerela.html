Kerala, India

Backwaters • Nature • Ayurveda • Peace

Overview

Kerala, known as “God’s Own Country”, is famous for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, hill stations, beaches and rich cultural heritage.

Best Time to Visit

September to March is the best time to visit Kerala due to pleasant weather.

Top Places to Visit

  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Munnar Hill Station
  • Wayanad
  • Kovalam Beach
  • Thekkady (Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary)

Famous Festivals

  • Onam
  • Vishu
  • Nehru Trophy Boat Race

Temples & Attractions

  • Sabarimala Temple
  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Fort Kochi
